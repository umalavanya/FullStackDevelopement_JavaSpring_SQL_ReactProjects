How to Play

  • Click and drag chess pieces to move them
  • Only valid moves according to chess rules are allowed
  • Blue squares show valid move locations
  • Red squares indicate capture moves
  • Use New Game to restart the board
  • Use Undo Move to take back your last move
  • Use Show Hint to see a suggested move

Special Moves: Castling and en passant are supported. Pawns promote to queens when reaching the opposite side.
